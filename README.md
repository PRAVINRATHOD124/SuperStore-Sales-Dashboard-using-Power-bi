# SuperStore-Sales-Dashboard-using-Power-bi
üìä Project Overview
This project features an interactive Sales Dashboard designed to track and analyze retail performance across multiple dimensions. The dashboard provides a high-level overview of key performance indicators (KPIs) while allowing users to drill down into regional, temporal, and category-specific data.
The primary goal of this project is to transform raw sales data into actionable business intelligence, helping stakeholders identify growth trends, optimize shipping logistics, and understand customer purchasing behavior.


üöÄ Key Features & KPIs
Executive Summary: Real-time tracking of Total Sales (1.6M), Profit (175K), Total Orders (22K), and Average Shipping Days (4).
Temporal Analysis: Year-over-Year (YoY) comparison of monthly Sales and Profit (2019 vs. 2020), highlighting seasonal peaks in the fourth quarter.
Market Segmentation: Breakdown of sales by Customer Segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office) and Payment Mode (COD, Online, Cards).
Geographical Insights: Regional distribution analysis showing the West region as a leading contributor (33% of sales).
Product Performance: Hierarchical view of Sales by Category (Office Supplies, Technology, Furniture) and top-performing Sub-Categories like Phones and Chairs.
Logistics Tracking: Analysis of shipping modes to monitor the preference for Standard Class shipping versus express options.

üìà Business Insights Derived
Seasonality: There is a significant spike in both sales and profit starting in September, peaking in December, suggesting successful holiday promotions.
Payment Trends: Cash on Delivery (COD) remains the most popular payment method (43%), indicating a potential area for incentivizing digital payments to improve cash flow.
Regional Strength: The West and East regions account for over 60% of total sales, suggesting these areas are prime candidates for expanded inventory or localized marketing.
Profitability: While Technology drives high sales value, Office Supplies maintain strong volume, showing a balanced product mix.
